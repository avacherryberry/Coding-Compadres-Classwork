What is ADHD?


According to wikipedia, "Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder is a neurodevelopmental disorder characterised by excessive amounts of inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity that are pervasive, impairing in multiple contexts, and otherwise age-inappropriate."
