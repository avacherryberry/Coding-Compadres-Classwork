Dont want to medicate? Dont fret!

Some people are hesitant to use pharmaceutical drugs, and others may have adverse reactions. Luckily, there are other ways to minimize the symptoms of this disorder.

Some things you may not know

  1. Many supplements, such as magnesium, are scientifically proven to substantially reduce symptoms.
  2. Pharmaceutical medications to treat ADHD have a high liklihood of being abused, and people with ADHD are 5-10x more likely to develope a substance abuse disorder, according to Web MD.

  • Create an exercise and nutrition plan. Refer to this article.
  • Practice mindfulness meditation.
  • Stock up on supplements.
